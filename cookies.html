This site may use cookies and similar technologies to provide core site functionality, measure performance, and (if enabled) serve advertising.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. They help websites remember preferences and understand usage patterns.

How we use cookies

  • Essential: site functionality and basic security controls.
  • Analytics (optional): aggregated metrics such as page views and browser type.
  • Advertising (future): if ads are enabled, third parties may use cookies to show relevant ads and measure results.

Managing cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain site features.
