How to add blogs on your own (simple manual method)

You do not need WordPress or a CMS right now. Keep it simple and professional with one file per article.

  1. 1Create a new file inside your /blog/ folder (example: mfa-rollout-mistakes.html).
  2. 2Copy your blog post template into that file and replace the title, date, meta tags, and article content.
  3. 3Open blog.html and add a new blog card at the top of the list with the correct link.
  4. 4Commit and push to GitHub. Cloudflare Pages redeploys automatically.
Example link: blog/mfa-rollout-mistakes.html
