Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Sentinel Identity (“we”, “us”) operates the website sentinelidentity.ca. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Contact/assessment form: name, email, organization (optional), selected topic (optional), and the issue summary you submit.
  • Basic site analytics (if enabled): aggregated usage data such as page views and device/browser type.
  • Cookies: used for site functionality and, if ads are enabled later, advertising/measurement cookies.

How we use information

  • To respond to your assessment request and communicate about your inquiry.
  • To improve site content and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent abuse/spam.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers used to operate the site (for example: hosting, email delivery, analytics, and later ad networks if you enable ads).

Advertising (future)

If we enable ads, third-party vendors may use cookies or device identifiers to serve ads and measure performance. You can control cookies in your browser settings.

Data retention

We retain form submissions for as long as needed to respond and maintain reasonable business records, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Your choices

  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your submitted information.
  • You can disable cookies in your browser (some site functions may be affected).
