Common tenant problems that quietly become big problems

Most environments “work” until a risky sign-in, over-permissioned admin account, or misconfigured access policy turns into downtime or a breach.

  • Too many admins, unclear privileged access model, and role sprawl over time.
  • MFA enabled, but not enforced correctly (legacy auth, weak methods, inconsistent coverage).
  • Conditional Access exists, but policies are incomplete, conflicting, or easy to bypass.
  • Guest and external sharing settings are open by default with no governance.
  • Hybrid identity drift: Entra Connect issues, unstable sync, surprise authentication behavior.
  • Azure subscription and Arc configuration gaps (ownership, policies, logging, security baselines).
